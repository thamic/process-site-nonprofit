Audience and Goals

Below are the audience and goals for Fronteering.com, a volunteer travel agency.

Audience

Below are the target audience members. I began with who is directly involved, and then branched out into who gives help, who needs help, critics, and fans. I found it interesting that my prmary audience will come out of the gives help category. Very little of the need help group would have the wifi or technology to access the website. In contrast, the give help group mainly fits a certain criteria- access to a substantial amount of funds, independent and mature of age, heart for both volunteering and travel, and willingess to work with travel agency.

Define Goals

Mission: To determine the audience, website, and client goals of Fronteering.com.

Overarching Goals

Client
  • Raise awareness about need and opportunities to solve need.
  • Attract traffic to website.
  • Gain new customers.
  • Connect with need-based opportunities.
  • Attract new volunteers and maintain old volunteers.
Audience
  • Gather information about fronteering.
  • Connect with Fronteering represenative.
  • Reach others through Fronteering.
  • Schedule a trip.
  • Gain new knowledge and experience.
Website
  • Make information easy to understand.
  • Make site easy to navigate.
  • Make attractive and eyecatching.
  • Make it easy to contact a representative.
  • Connect with other travel agencies/companies to get the word out.
  • Make bookings easy to make.
  • Make the travel location/type easy to find.

Specific Goals

User Goal Content/Action
Travelers Choose Fronteering over other similar companies. Travel. Share experience. Log in, become a "member of the team", read a review, book a stay, option to donate, see locations and opprtunity descriptions.
Donators Give money. Have opportunity to host a traveler or give directly to program.
Experienced Rsearchers/Scientists/Educators Become staff. Application available to apply to be a member of the leadership team.
